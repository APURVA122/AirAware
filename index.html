Key Features

🗺️

Localized AQI Visualizer

Interactive map displaying current Air Quality Index for rural and smaller urban locations with 1 km² resolution using ISRO satellite data.

🌧️

Integrated Forecasting

3-day AQI forecast and 1-week rain prediction crucial for agricultural planning and daily activities in rural areas.

🏥

Health Alert System

Personalized health advisories for families with specific guidance for children, elderly, and vulnerable populations.

🎤

Voice-Enabled Kheti Mitra

Interactive chatbot supporting Hindi and regional languages, helping farmers with voice-based queries and agricultural advice.

🌾

Crop Suggestion Panel

Tailored crop advice based on current air quality, weather conditions, and seasonal data for informed farming decisions.

📱

SMS & WhatsApp Alerts

Critical AQI warnings, health tips, and farming advice delivered via SMS and WhatsApp for maximum reach in rural areas.

🛡️

Smart Precaution Engine

Automatically suggests precautions based on detected AQI levels and weather conditions with multilingual support.

🏫

School/Community Dashboard

Simplified dashboard for local schools and community centers to monitor air quality and receive general advisories.
